EVEXIA

a calming app for a calm mind

Evexia aims to aid individuals dealing with daily anxiety or stress. Our application provides resources that allow users to track their emotions, mental health and personal experiences. Evexia also provides users with resources to ameliorate their mindfulness. In addition, our application has a Thinking Game that helps individuals relieve their anxiety.
